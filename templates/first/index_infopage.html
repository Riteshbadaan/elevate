Cricket

Rules and Regulations:
1. The player should be a student of an engineering college.
2. Umpire’s decision is final and mandatory for all.
3. Participant should be born after 1 Jan 1993 (below 18 years of age not allowed).
4. Students with current year drop are not eligible.
5. The participants must bring the following documents:
• Valid Original college ID card(with college stamp on it)
• Bonafide signed and stamped by principal on college letter head with player’s photo on it.
• Fee receipt of current academic year.[In case objection is raised three national ID’s and previous semester/year mark sheet will be required.]
6. Document verification is last step for confirmation of registration of team/player.
7. Any fault in the document verification leads to disqualification of team/player.
8. Each team will get the match ball from PICT.
9. Each innings will be of 20 overs.
10. Team list of 16 must submitted during registration.
11. Whites are compulsory for every match.
12. In case of a tie, the winner will be decided on wickets fallen. If further there is a tie, then the winner will be decided on the basis of a bowl out.

Boys: ₹ 7500

Prize Money:
Winner: ₹ 20000
Runner Up: ₹ 15000

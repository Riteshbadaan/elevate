ABOUT ELEVATE

Elevate began its inter-corporate chapter in the year 2014 and since then it has been one of the most sought-after sports events in the city. As the event is distributed over two weekends, it gives teams the exact sports getaway they look forward to for catering to their sports fanaticism.
Once a sportsman, always a sportsman. This probably came up because of a common phenomenon all sportsman share, that is- ‘The Runners High’- with a spike in the amount of endorphins in the blood, every player who has been playing a certain sport for more than 3 years has eventually found a way to push his or her limits. The sportsman spirit is thus born.
